1: Using center to locate the image at center.

Center image with size control

2: Using single line to display image.

Center image by single line . Put link/source to the right.

3: Using Group Pictures to put mutiple images.

A fully filled center image by GP , with 1 image and layout 1.

A grouped center image by GP , with 4 images and layout 3.
For layout usages, check https://theme-next.js.org/docs/tag-plugins/group-pictures

Upload a Post

To upload a post, find the root directory of the blog files. Then press the right mouse button.
